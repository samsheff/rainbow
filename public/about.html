We Collect Beautiful Colors

Color Hunt is a place to discover and share beautiful color palettes. We pick and collect curated color combinations that has a magic. Our colors are all yours, you are welcome to use, share, and get inspiration, every day.

We created Color Hunt to celebrate the beauty of colors in life, and each palette is defined with it's colors by digital HEX codes. Our palettes are being used by web designers, art directors, fashion designers, interior designers, illustrators and many other people which are looking for some beautiful color combinations.

We Accept Palette Suggestions

Color Hunt is a social platform which is updated daily. We love to enrich our collection with color palettes created by you. Our users create beautiful palettes every day. Each creation is being reviewed by our hunters team, and the very best of those get featured in our home page. Give it a try and contribute to our collection.

Join The Hunters

We always keep on posting new color inspirations on Color Hunt's Facebook page and also on our Twitter account. Follow us, share the collection with your friends, and drop us a line with your feedback! [hello@colorhunt.co] We would love to hear from you!
